[[principles]]
= Principles and prerequisites

This chapter describes the principles and assumptions that underlie the use of PEPPOL Invoice and Credit Note. It is
based on {EN16931} and its corresponding set of documents.

This BIS will identify, explain and justify the business requirements for the Invoice and Credit Note. It
provides syntax bindings to OASIS UBL 2.1. It also includes a syntax implementation guide.
